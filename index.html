ARCHAEOLOGICAL SITE
OF NALANDA MAHAVIHARA
AT NALANDA, BIHAR

Introduction

The NALANDA MAHAVIHARA site is in the state of BIHAR, in north eastern India.

Time Period

It has the archaeological remains of a monastic and scolastic institution in the Period of 3rd century BCE to the 13th century CE.

Archaeological Structure

It includes stupas , shrines , Viharas ,aan important art works in stucco , stone and metal.
